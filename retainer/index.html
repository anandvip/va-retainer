Payment Terms

  • Payment Amount: The monthly retainer fee is CAD $400, payable in advance each month.
  • Payment Due Date: Payment is due on or before the 5th day of each month to ensure uninterrupted services.
  • Payment Methods: Payments can be made via bank transfer, credit card, or online payment platforms such as Remitely (to be confirmed).
  • Invoice: An invoice will be issued at least 7 days before the payment due date.
  • Late Payment: If payment is not received by the due date, a late fee of 2% per month on the outstanding balance will be applied.
  • Suspension of Services: Services may be suspended if payment is more than 15 days overdue, with prior notice.
  • Refunds: The retainer fee is non-refundable. Any unused hours do not roll over to the next month.
  • Scope Changes: If the scope of work increases significantly, the retainer fee may be adjusted with mutual agreement.
  • Contract Renewal: The retainer agreement will automatically renew monthly unless either party provides written notice of termination at least 15 days prior to the next billing cycle.

Note

This retainer is designed to be fair, sustainable, and scalable. We can revisit the fee if the scope of work increases significantly over time. add or modify any clause you think is inappropriate for our arrangement.
